**v2 — Calligraphic transition.** Cinema intro becomes three theatrical beats → wax seal → curtain reveal. The morpher becomes a calligraphic SVG tracing of **دار** (ra → alif → dal, with diacritic dot) drawn live on a stroke‑dasharray timeline; English variants whisper around the edges. Custom gold cursor with a saffron dust trail. Scroll‑driven section reveals on long easings. Mouse‑parallax on the arch.

**v3 — WebGL & ambient audio & culture.** Real Three.js arch tunnel — extruded horseshoe arches receding into FogExp2 darkness, gold trim rings, dust motes drifting in a spotlight shaft, scroll‑driven dolly forward, mouse‑parallax look. Web Audio drone (D2 + A2 + D3 + low D detuned with slow LFOs through a feedback‑delay reverb), one gold toggle, soft chimes on swatch and house hover. Three signature interactions, one per culture:
- **Beiruti** — drag horizontally to throw open the wood shutters and reveal the courtyard.
- **Najdi** — multi‑layer parallax majlis: lanterns and cushions move at different depths.
- **Riad** — be still. Stop moving and the fountain ripples bloom; nudge and they fade.

**v4 — A film, a house, a word.** Cinema intro re‑timed (8.4s) with grain overlay, traced gold seal stroke, skip button, longer letter‑spacing breath on the title cards. Coda accepts a real photo, fades it in behind the dashed card at .55 opacity, plays a 440 → 523 → 659 chime arpeggio.

Four more iterations shipped, each one breaking what came before:

**v5 — One continuous shot.** Section model deleted. The visitor never "navigates" between acts — they ride a single Catmull‑Rom curve through one persistent space, scroll mapped to camera dolly with smoothed inertia. Time of day lerps continuously across five waypoints (dusk → evening → night → adhan → dawn) feeding fog, ambient, key‑light color, and one quiet witness label at lower‑left. The **ر** is born during the load trace and stays on screen until coda — eight hand‑authored SVG keyframes (seal stroke → arch keystone → tea‑pour S‑curve → lantern pendulum → tunnel keystone → water sine → swallow's path → keyhole keystone → upload underline), tweened command‑by‑command so the actor never disappears. Loading is the first act: stroke‑offset is tied to actual asset readiness, no skip button. Pull the seal downward during boot and a Hijri colophon (مُصمَّمٌ في دار · سَنَة ١٤٤٧ هـ) lifts.

**v6 — Commitment.** The three houses become a single threshold gesture in `[0.06, 0.16]` of the journey: a central seal, three doorways (بيروت / نَجد / فاس), draw a thread to one. Release on a door = commit. The choice writes `data-house` on `<html>` and re‑pigments **everything downstream**: gold material color, dust hue, drone fundamental + detune drift + sway LFO, Arabic font weight, English emphasis weight/style, motif tempo. The other two doors don't disappear — they fade to 18% and pass as architecture the camera doesn't take. `localStorage` persists the choice; replay = different film.

**v7 — Sound, scent, breath.** Detuned sines replaced by **Karplus‑Strong plucked strings** — short noise burst into a feedback‑delay tuned to frequency, lowpass on the loop, peaking‑filter "body" — that actually reads as a struck oud string, looped quietly across each house's tonal triad. **Per‑chamber convolution reverb** with procedurally‑generated impulse responses: courtyard 1.0s with an early slap reflection, corridor 1.6s, tunnel 2.4s, atrium 4.8s, riad 6.0s — IRs swap as the camera crosses zone boundaries (and a footstep marks each crossing). **Footsteps** also fire on scroll cadence, gain scaled to scroll velocity. **Fountain** is filtered pink noise, gain modulated by mouse stillness — only audible in the riad chamber, only when you stop moving. **Adhan** plays once at p ≈ .82: three sustained vowel tones with formant peaking‑filter at 720/1180 Hz, vibrato‑detuned, panned far left. **Mobile**: gyro pitch/roll feeds camera lookAt offsets (the world tilts like a brass tray). **Long‑press** at p ≥ .72 reveals a scent — نعناع/mint, زهر/orange blossom, عَنبَر/amber, وَرد/rose — with a haptic ripple `[12, 40, 18]` and a soft chime.

**v8 — The portal.** The coda is no longer an upload card. The visitor drops a room into the keyhole — and the camera does not stop. A `MeshBasicMaterial` plane sized to the photo's aspect ratio sits inside the keyhole at z≈‑91.4. On drop, a `portaling` flag flips: the camera dollies forward into the plane over 4.2s with eased timing, FOV narrows from 56° → 24°, fog density doubles, dust accelerates inward as if drawn through the keyhole, a gold viewfinder ring scales from 0 → 2.0 around it, and at portalP > .80 a radial white→gold flash blooms. The photo's now full‑viewport — that's the camera the next page inherits. A cubic chime arpeggio (C5 → E5 → G5 → C6) resolves on the bloom. `location.href = '/transform'` fires at portalP = 1, but the visual transition is already complete; the navigation is invisible underneath the gold.

All four registered side‑by‑side. v8 is the floor.
